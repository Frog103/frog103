Project: Unite

About This Product

Developer: StickyBell09

Publisher: TBD

Release Date: TBD

Genre: RPG, Casual, 2D, Exploration, Puzzle, Singleplayer

Embark on a whimsical yet perilous adventure into the mysterious worlds of the Cradles: portals to vibrant dimensions teeming with magic, monsters, and secrets. Follow the story of veteran adventurer Ram and rookie Nonetto, as they navigate various areas, and work through mind-bending puzzles.

But survival wont be just about brute strength. Your success hinges on strategy and teamwork in the innovative Unite Battle System, an interesting twist on the classic turn-based format.

Unite Battle System

Live and Die Together:

Your party’s life is unified. Damage, healing, and buffs are shared, forcing you to think tactically about every decision. One reckless move could spell disaster for the entire group.

Risk vs. Reward:

Unlock powerful abilities as characters reach critical health thresholds—but beware, this comes at the cost of increasing their vulnerability.

Dynamic Targeting:

Control the flow of battle by manipulating enemy behavior, shifting attention between party members with aggro-based skills, or by carefully balancing their actions.

Unite Meter:

Build synergy across the team to unleash devastating attacks and tide-turning buffs. Plan your strategy, execute your moves, and push the limits of your party's potential.

Evolving Puzzles:

Encounter unique puzzles that integrate directly with the game’s narrative, from clever riddles to sprawling multi-step contraptions that challenge your mind as much as your reflexes. Your choices in solving puzzles ripple through the story, unlocking new opportunities and secrets as you dive deeper into the Cradle's mysteries.

A Story That Unfolds Across Dimensions:

Explore richly detailed worlds within the Cradles, each with its own compelling story arc, characters, and conflicts.
